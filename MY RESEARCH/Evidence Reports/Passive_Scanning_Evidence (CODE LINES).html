⏱️ _rate_limit_passive() – Polite scanning throttle

def _rate_limit_passive(self):
    if self.config.passive_scanning and self.config.passive_rate_limit > 0:
        with self.rate_limit_semaphore:
            elapsed = time.time() - self.last_request_time
            if elapsed < self.config.passive_rate_limit:
                time.sleep(self.config.passive_rate_limit - elapsed)
            self.last_request_time = time.time()
✅ Passive evidence: The method introduces a deliberate delay between requests (1 second default) — a hallmark of passive scanning to avoid overwhelming servers and to remain unaggressive.
This behavior is not typical of active scanners, which send bursts of requests.
